Employment Contract

This Employment Contract ("Contract") is made and entered into on [INSERT: Date of Execution], by and between:

Acme Fintech LLC, a company duly registered under the laws of the United Arab Emirates, with trade license number [INSERT: Trade License Number], having its principal place of business in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (hereinafter referred to as the "Employer");

AND

[INSERT: Full Name of Employee], holder of passport number [INSERT: Passport Number], residing at [INSERT: Employee Address] (hereinafter referred to as the "Employee").

Collectively referred to as the "Parties" and individually as a "Party".

1. Position and Duties

1.1The Employer hereby employs the Employee, and the Employee hereby accepts employment, as a Senior Software Engineer.

1.2The Employee shall perform such duties and responsibilities as are normally associated with the position and as may be assigned from time to time by the Employer's management, including but not limited to software development, system architecture design, code reviews, and collaboration with cross-functional teams.

1.3The Employee shall devote their full professional time, skill, and attention to the performance of duties and shall act in the best interests of the Employer at all times.

2. Term of Employment

2.1This Contract shall commence on [INSERT: Start Date] and shall continue unless terminated in accordance with the provisions herein.

2.2The initial period of employment shall include a probationary period of six (6) months, during which either Party may terminate this Contract by providing thirty (30) days' written notice.

3. Probation Period and Review

3.1During the probation period, the Employee's performance will be subject to formal review at the end of the third month.

3.2Based on the outcome of the review, the Employer shall determine whether the Employee's services will be confirmed for permanent employment.

3.3Confirmation of employment after probation does not alter the at-will nature of the employment relationship, subject to applicable notice requirements.

4. Compensation and Benefits

4.1The Employee shall receive a monthly gross salary of AED 32,000 (Thirty-Two Thousand UAE Dirhams), payable in arrears on or before the fifth (5th) day of each succeeding month via bank transfer.

4.2In addition, the Employer shall provide a housing allowance of AED 6,000 per month and a transport allowance of AED 1,500 per month.

4.3The Employee shall be entitled to thirty (30) calendar days of paid annual leave per year of continuous service, accruing pro rata.

5. Non-Compete and Confidentiality

5.1For a period of twelve (12) months following termination of employment, the Employee shall not, within the United Arab Emirates, directly or indirectly engage in any fintech business which competes with the Employer.

5.2The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information, trade secrets, and business strategies of the Employer, both during and after the term of employment.

6. End of Service Benefits

6.1Upon termination of employment, the Employee shall be entitled to end-of-service gratuity calculated in accordance with the UAE Labour Law, based on basic salary and years of continuous service.
